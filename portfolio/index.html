MyClinic is a certified healthcare web app by Breathment, which acts as a companion app for health professionals, specifically COPD specialists and physiotherapists. The app enables health professionals to monitor the progress of their patients, as well as define customized treatment programs.
The performance of patients is analyzed by an AI trainer which is deployed, along with the rest of the app, with the help of a CI/CD pipeline for the sake of scalability.

Features

  • Detailed dashboard with multiple sort and search features
  • Extensive details view and actions for each patient
  • AI trainer to monitor and analyze patient progress
  • Customizable treatment programs and patient categories
